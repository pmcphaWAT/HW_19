My UX Skills

User Research

The most important aspect of design is user research. I believe good product design is driven by insightful qualitative and quantitative research. I've performed research through various outlets including surveys and interviews.

User Interface Design

One of my favorite aspects of the UX design process is ideating and prototyping designs. I start my UI designs by simple pen and pencil before producing low fidelity wireframes. Lastly, I evolve the low fidelity designs into high fidelity interactive prototypes.

Usability Testing

In order to validate my designs and ensure that they meet user expectations, usability testing is key. I perform various forms of testing including guerilla testing, 5-second testing and, AB testing.
